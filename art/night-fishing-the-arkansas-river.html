NIGHT-FISHING THE ARKANSAS RIVER

COLLECTION: CHARACTER

This is the quietest piece in the Case Study 42 archive, and possibly the most revealing. Broboticus stands alone at the river's edge — no city, no dojo, no kitchen stadium — just the crescent moon, a striped vaporwave sky in every shade of blue from midnight to cyan, and a fishing line disappearing into still water. His eyes glow red in the dark. The horizontal banding of the background gives the whole scene the feel of a CRT screen tuned to somewhere peaceful. He is not in a hurry. Field Report 11 contains only one observation: Broboticus finds the water calming. No further data recorded.
