LOVE OF THE GAME

COLLECTION: SURREALISM

The uniform is correct in every detail — cap, jersey, belt, batting gloves — and the BasicGlitch treatment has left those details legible while replacing everything else with light. The figure is outlined in red and gold, the face rendered in warm tones that emerge from the neon edge detection like a portrait coming through static. From the center of the chest: a starburst, cyan and yellow and green, not added as decoration but as revelation — as if the process of running this image through the BasicGlitch treatment revealed something that was already there and just needed the right filter to become visible. Love of the game doesn't look like anything you can teach. It looks like this.
