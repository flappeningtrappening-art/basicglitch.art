DISCWORLD

SERIES: Hand-Drawn Ink

Terry Pratchett's cosmology, rendered by hand in ink with the devotion of a true believer. Great A'Tuin swims through a hand-stippled cosmos carrying four elephants carrying a disc world. Every surface is patterned. Every element is resolved. This took exactly as long as it looks like it took. The turtle shell mapped in precise geometric diamond patterns, the four elephants individually textured, the disc world itself rendered in flowing topographic line work. Around everything: dot trails, celestial markers, the suggestion of infinite space handled with the same patience and specificity as the central figures.
