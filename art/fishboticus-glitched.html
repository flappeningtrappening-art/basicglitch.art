FISHBOTICUS GLITCHED

COLLECTION: SURREALISM

Take Night-Fishing the Arkansas River — its calm vaporwave stripes, its quiet midnight energy, Broboticus patient at the water's edge — and run it through a system that's starting to fail. The stripes have fractured into reds and purples and circuit-traced patterns. The fishing rod bleeds red where it crosses into the corrupted zones. A red border has appeared around the entire frame like a warning that wasn't there before. Broboticus himself has gone warm — outlined now in gold and cream rather than the cool white of the original — and remains completely still through all of it. The glitch is everywhere except him. He is the stable element in an unstable system, which may be the most accurate thing that's ever been depicted about this character. The line is still in the water. He is still waiting.
