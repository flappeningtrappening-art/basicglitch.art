SANGRE DE CRISTOS — NIGHT

COLLECTION: AVAILABLE FOR PURCHASE · SANGRE DE CRISTOS

Colorado's Sangre de Cristo range, rendered in two colors and zero compromise. Pure neon green on absolute black — the mountain forms stripped to their essential geometry, the circuit board rising from the valley between peaks as traces of light against the dark sky. Small orbs drift through the upper darkness like data packets in transit. The river traces its path through the foreground in careful contour lines. This is the piece that began the series — the one that proved the concept worked. Standing before these specific mountains in Colorado and processing them through the BasicGlitch visual language produced something true. Every other variant is a conversation with this one. The mountains were always transmitting. This is where BasicGlitch first heard it.
