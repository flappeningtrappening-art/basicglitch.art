APEX RENDERING

COLLECTION: CYBER-ECLECTIC

The mane is the whole argument — red at the roots, burning through orange to gold at the tips, shot through with cyan pixels where the glitch has found its way in. He sits with the absolute confidence of something that has never once considered whether it belongs, in a landscape that has clearly decided to become something new. Behind him: dark rock formations outlined in purple neon, trees that have gone partially digital, a crescent moon rebuilt from circuit board traces. The pixel dissolution happening across his body isn't damage — it's translation. He is becoming data without losing a single ounce of authority.
