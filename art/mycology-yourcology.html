MYCOLOGY YOURCOLOGY

COLLECTION: SURREALISM

Where A Mycological Phenomenon presents its mushroom as a singular, quietly powerful presence under a star-dotted night, Mycology Yourcology turns the dial to a different frequency entirely. The sky here is all motion — deep blue and purple swirling in Van Gogh spirals, as if the atmosphere itself is responding to something the mushroom is transmitting. The cap of the Amanita has changed too: it wears spiral whorl patterns now, concentric and hypnotic, like the mushroom grew eyes while you weren't looking. Every surface maintains the dense embroidered stitch texture that defines this series, but the energy is wilder, more awake. The two pieces share a world. This one knows you're in it.
