ABOUT THE ARTIST

The Digital Visionary: BasicGlitch

For BasicGlitch, the digital canvas began with a simple revelation: the power of the UNDO. This freedom to iterate without fear allowed for the evolution of a complex, signature line style that bridges the gap between structured reality and digital chaos. Known in the physical world as Justin Lance, the persona BasicGlitch crafts visions that refuse to be boring—rejecting traditional norms in favor of high-saturation Tech-Noir environments and playful cinematic homages.

This work is deeply informed by a journey across the American landscape. From roots in Newburgh, Indiana to the life-altering experience of standing before Colorado’s 14ers, BasicGlitch learned that true understanding only comes through experience. This philosophy continues into the New Mexico desert, where beauty is found in the unexpected bloom of a cactus or the stark, bold geometry of a mountain peak.

Off-screen, the artist is a musician of 31 years, finding a common frequency between the intricate rhythms of 90s electronic pulses and the vibrant color palettes of the art. Whether cooking to bring people together or hiking into the silence of the high peaks, the mission is to create a positive, introspective experience—a neon light against the world's negativity.
