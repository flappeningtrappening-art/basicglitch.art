ABOUT THE ARTIST

The Digital Visionary: Justin Lance

For Justin Lance, the digital canvas began with a simple revelation: the power of the UNDO. This freedom to iterate without fear allowed him to evolve a complex, signature line style that bridges the gap between structured reality and digital chaos. Operating under the persona BasicGlitch, Justin crafts visions that refuse to be boring—rejecting traditional norms in favor of high-saturation Tech-Noir environments and playful cinematic homages.

His work is deeply informed by a journey across the American landscape. From his roots in Indiana to the life-altering experience of standing before Colorado’s 14ers, Justin learned that true understanding only comes through experience. This philosophy follows him into the New Mexico desert, where he finds beauty in the unexpected bloom of a cactus or the stark, bold geometry of a mountain peak.

Off-screen, Justin is a musician of 31 years, finding a common frequency between the intricate rhythms of 90s electronic pulses and the vibrant color palettes of his art. Whether he is cooking to bring people together or hiking into the silence of the high peaks, his mission is to create a positive, introspective experience—a neon light against the world's negativity.
