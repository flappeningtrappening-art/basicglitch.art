TERMS & LICENSING

Legal Framework for Digital & Physical Assets

1. OWNERSHIP & COPYRIGHT

Unless otherwise stated in a written contract, Justin Lance (BasicGlitch) retains full copyright of all artwork created. The client is granted a non-exclusive license to use the artwork based on the tier purchased.

2. USAGE LICENSES

  • Personal Use: High-resolution files for wallpapers, social profile icons, and single physical prints for personal display. No resale or commercial integration.
  • Commercial License: Grants the right to use the art for branding, album covers, book covers, and marketing. Redistribution for direct profit (selling the art itself as prints/merch) requires an additional "Commercial Buyout" agreement.

3. PAYMENT & COMMISSIONS

Work on custom commissions begins only after payment is confirmed. For high-tier projects (Quantum & Singularity), a 50% non-refundable deposit may be arranged via contact.

4. REVISIONS POLICY

Revisions are defined as minor adjustments to color, lighting, or specific small details. Fundamental changes to the composition or subject after work has reached the rendering phase may incur additional "Redrafting" fees.

5. REFUNDS & CANCELLATIONS

Due to the digital nature of the products and the labor involved, no refunds will be issued once work has commenced. If a project is cancelled by the artist, a full refund will be provided.

6. AI POLICY

Basic Glitch Art is handcrafted digital art. We do not use generative AI to create the core compositions or characters in our commissions, ensuring your art is a unique artifact of human creativity.
