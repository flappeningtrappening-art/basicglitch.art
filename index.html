About

Basic.Glitch

Basic.Glitch is the persona of Justin Lance, an Indiana native that relocated to various parts of Colorado then to Otero County, New Mexico. My experiences give birth to my art. I blend neon line-art, psychedelic surrealism, textured thread-painted landscapes, and nature-fusion portraits. Pieces are optimized for prints and digital displays. I always have been and always will be a basic glitch.
